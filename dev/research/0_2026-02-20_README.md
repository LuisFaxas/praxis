# Research

Upstream knowledge gathering. Research informs planning — gather information here before making decisions in `planning/`.

## Pipeline Position

```
Research  →  Planning  →  Execution  →  Reports
(gather)    (decide)     (build)       (communicate)
  ^^^
 YOU ARE HERE
```

## Structure

- `active/` — Research for current or upcoming decisions
- `archive/` — Referenced research where the decision has already been made

## Lifecycle

Research does NOT follow the draft/published pipeline. It is internal-only.

1. AI writes research to `active/`
2. Research is referenced during planning (master plans, work orders)
3. When the decision is made, research stays as historical reference
4. Admin moves completed research to `archive/` during periodic cleanup

## When to Use

- Before creating a master plan — research the problem space first
- Before choosing a technology — compare options here
- Before a major architectural decision — document tradeoffs
- External research (pricing, benchmarks, best practices, documentation)
